Rubellite

Known for its vibrant pink to red hues, rubellite is a variety of tourmaline that captivates the heart.

Gemology Facts

Rubellite is a rare and precious variety of tourmaline that ranges in color from pink to red.

Historic Significance

Rubellite has been used in fine jewelry for centuries. It was often mistaken for ruby and has adorned royal crowns and scepters.
