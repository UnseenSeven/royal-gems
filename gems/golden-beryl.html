Golden Beryl

Also known as heliodor, this gemstone is renowned for its golden-yellow hues.

Gemology Facts

Golden beryl is a yellow variety of beryl. Its name, heliodor, comes from the Greek words for "sun" and "gift."

Historic Significance

Golden beryl has been treasured for its sunny hues and has been used in various forms of jewelry and adornments throughout history.
