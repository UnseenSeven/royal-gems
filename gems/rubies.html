Rubies

These precious gemstones are renowned for their vibrant red color.

Gemology Facts

Rubies are a variety of the mineral corundum. They get their red color from traces of chromium.

Historic Significance

Rubies have been prized for their beauty and rarity for centuries. They have been used in crowns, jewelry, and other precious items.
