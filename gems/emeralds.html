Emeralds

Known for their captivating green color, often associated with royalty and beauty.

Gemology Facts

Emeralds are a variety of the mineral beryl. Their green color is due to trace amounts of chromium and sometimes vanadium.

Historic Significance

Emeralds have been used in jewelry and as talismans for centuries. They were especially prized by ancient Egyptians and Inca kings.
