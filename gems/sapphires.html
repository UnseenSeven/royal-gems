Sapphires

Symbolizing wisdom and purity, these gems were lost at sea for centuries.

Gemology Facts

Sapphires are a variety of the mineral corundum. They come in various colors, but the blue sapphire is the most famous and valuable.

Historic Significance

Sapphires have been cherished for thousands of years. They were believed to protect their wearers from envy and harm.
