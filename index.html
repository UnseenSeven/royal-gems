Our Story

In the golden age of exploration, Sir Francis Drake sailed the seas, amassing treasures that became legends. Centuries later, @TheKingofJewelz and his father, D’Angelo (Action Jackson), embarked on their own quest. Bound by love and adventure, they uncovered gems lost to the ocean’s depths—each a testament to nature’s artistry and their unbreakable bond. Today, @TheKingofJewelz honors his father’s legacy by sharing these timeless relics with the world.
