Our Story

Royal Gems was founded by @TheKingofJewelz, inspired by the adventures and passion of his late father, D'Angelo Jackson, also known as Action Jackson. D'Angelo served his country with distinction in the Air Force, as a firefighter, and as a correctional officer for the State of California. In his last golden years, father and son embarked on treasure hunts, uncovering stories and histories behind their discoveries. From hidden gold to the legendary treasures of Sir Francis Drake, which we secured through correspondence with the Queen and Buckingham Palace, our journey is filled with historic significance and awe.
